HOW TO USE THE TEXAS VALUE ENGINE
How To Use
  1. Analyze Value: Navigate to the Arb tab to find mathematical values.
  2. Lock Your Core: Tap the + button next to any player to lock them into your roster.
  3. Auto-Fill: Navigate to the Build tab and tap "Auto-Fill" to automatically surround your core with the best mathematical plays.
  4. Save & Copy: Your work is saved automatically. Use the Copy button to export your lineup.
Glossary / Key terms
Δ (Delta): Salary Rank minus Odds Rank. Positive numbers mean the player is cheaper than their win probability suggests.
Vel (Velocity): Real-time betting line movement. ▲ means money is coming in on that player.
$DLTA: The master value metric. Blends Odds, Delta, Velocity, and Course Fit into one score.
Play Responsibly

Daily Fantasy Golf is high variance. Practice strict bankroll management.

  • The 10% Rule: Never play more than 10% of your total bankroll in one week.
  • The 80/20 Split: Allocate 80% to Cash games (50/50s) and 20% to GPPs (Tournaments).
