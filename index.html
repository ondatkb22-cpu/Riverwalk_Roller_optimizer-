★ How to Use This Tool

The math is already done for you.

  1. Grab an Optimal: Tap the PRE tab. The engine builds 3 mathematically perfect lineups based on the latest live odds.
  2. Hunt for Tacos: Check the ARB tab to see who is taking sharp money (🌮) and outperforming their salary.
  3. Build Your Own: Lock your favorite core plays on the ARB tab (+), then go to the BUILD tab and hit "Auto-Fill Optimal Lineup".
★ Quant Glossary
  • Δ (Delta): Salary Rank vs. Odds Rank. Positive means DK mispriced them to your advantage.
  • wVEL (Multi-Market): Blended Time-Decay Velocity. Tracks sharp money across Outright, T5, and T10 markets.
  • $DLTA: Master EV score. Blends Win/T5/T10 odds with Course History, Strokes Gained, and Line Movement.
  • pOWN: Projected Ownership. Dynamically tracks leverage based on mathematical value and sharp steam.
