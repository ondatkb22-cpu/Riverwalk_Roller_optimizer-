User Guide
HOW TO USE THE TEXAS VALUE ENGINE
Step-by-Step Instructions
  1. Analyze the Arb Tab: Review the field sorted by our master value metric ($DLTA) to find golfers whose DraftKings salaries are mispriced compared to their win probability and course fit.
  2. Lock Your Core: Tap the + button next to a player to lock them into your roster. Keep an eye on your remaining salary in the bottom HUD strip.
  3. Build Your Lineup: Switch to the Build tab. Select Cash or GPP, set your wave stacking preference (AM/PM), and tap "Auto-Fill Lineup" to calculate the mathematically optimal lineup around your locks.
  4. Review Pre-Built: Need quick action? The Pre-Built tab automatically generates optimal Cash, GPP, and Line-Movement (Steam) rosters for the current field.
Glossary / Key terms
Δ (Delta): Salary Rank minus Odds Rank. A positive number means a player is cheaper than their win probability suggests.
Vel (Velocity): Real-time betting line movement. "▲" means odds are shortening (improving).
$DLTA: The Engine's Master Score. Blends Odds, Delta, Velocity, Cut Equity, and Course Fit into one number.
Conviction: LOCK (>8 score), STRONG (>5), CONSIDER (>2), FADE (<2 or neg Delta).
Fit: Course fit specifically evaluated for TPC San Antonio (Greg Norman design, high winds, Poa Trivialis).
Bankroll & Disclaimer

Daily Fantasy Golf is high variance. Practice strict bankroll management to ensure long-term profitability.

  • The 10% Rule: Never put more than 10% of your total bankroll into play in a single week.
