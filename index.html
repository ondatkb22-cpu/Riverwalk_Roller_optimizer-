How to Use This Tool

The math is already done for you.

  1. Grab an Optimal: Tap the PRE tab at the bottom. The engine has automatically built 3 mathematically perfect lineups (Cash, GPP, Steam) using live odds and course history.
  2. Hunt for Tacos: Check the ARB tab to see who is taking sharp money (🌮) and outperforming their DraftKings salary.
  3. Build Your Own: Want to force a player into your lineup? Tap the + next to their name on the ARB tab, go to the BUILD tab, and hit "Auto-Fill Optimal Lineup".
Quant Glossary

Understanding the Engine's Metrics

  • Δ (Delta): This measures pure price-tag value. It compares a player's DraftKings Salary Rank to their Betting Odds Rank. If a player is the 20th most expensive but has the 10th best odds to win, their Delta is +10. A positive number means they are mathematically underpriced by DraftKings.
  • $DLTA: This is the engine's ultimate "Expected Value" score. It blends a player's Win, Top 5, and Top 10 odds with their Course History and Sharp Line Movement (Steam), and calculates exactly how many fantasy points the player should score versus their salary. A positive $DLTA means the player is projected to vastly outscore their price tag.
